Biography

Currently I am an associate professor of School of Computer Science in Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications.


Research Interests

  • Data Mining for Numerical Data, Mixed Data and Categorical Data

  • Data Science for Sports Analytics

  • Natural Language Processing for Chinese Classics Digitization and Games

  • Swarm Intelligence Optimization for Machine Learning


Professional Services

  • PC Member:  PAKDD 2019, PAKDD 2018 and PAKDD 2017.
